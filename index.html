About

I grew up on Stroms Farm near Guelph, Ontario, which my brother runs today. I studied Water Resource Engineering at the University of Guelph and went on to a master's at the University of Waterloo in air pollution dispersion modelling.

These days I live in southern Alberta with my family. I work on hard, interesting problems by day and spend the rest of my time building things on our own piece of land.
